Short Description¶

Description of WAVEFORM namelist

Namelist Variables¶

  • NCTRL Number of control points

  • TCTRL List of control times (ms)

  • ICTRL List of control currents (kA)

  • PCTRL List of relative phases of RMP coil currents (units of PI)
